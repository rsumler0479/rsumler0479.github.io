Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime, with changes taking effect at the next billing cycle. No penalties or cancellation fees.

What if my business grows significantly?

As your business scales, you can upgrade to a higher tier or move to a custom Enterprise plan. We'll work with you to ensure your plan matches your needs.

Do you offer a free trial?

We offer a free 20-minute consultation to discuss your needs and show you how DARES works. After that, you can start with a Starter plan on a month-to-month basis.

Are there setup fees?

No setup or hidden fees. We include onboarding, integration setup, and your first month of service at your plan's monthly rate.

What about year-end taxes?

All plans include year-end tax preparation support and documentation. Enterprise plans include dedicated tax optimization planning.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. We believe in month-to-month flexibility. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No contracts, no penalties.
