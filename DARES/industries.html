Construction Companies

DARES provides comprehensive bookkeeping solutions specifically designed for residential, commercial, and specialty construction firms.

Construction Accounting Challenges We Solve

Construction companies face unique accounting challenges including:

  • Multi-Project Tracking – Track costs across multiple simultaneous projects
  • Job Costing Complexity – Categorize expenses by trade, labor, and materials
  • Subcontractor Management – Manage payments and 1099 reporting
  • Lien Waiver Compliance – Maintain compliance with lien law requirements
  • Certificate of Insurance – Track and manage insurance requirements from subcontractors
  • Progress Billing – Invoice based on percentage of completion
  • Equipment & Vehicle Tracking – Monitor asset depreciation and maintenance
