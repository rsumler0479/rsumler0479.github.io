The Best of Automation + Human Expertise

Our 4-step process combines cutting-edge AI with real human accountants to give you accuracy you can trust.

How The Magic Happens

1

Data Sync

We connect to your financial ecosystem in seconds.

DARES securely integrates with your bank, QuickBooks, Xero, Bill.com, Procore, AppFolio, or any accounting software you use. Data flows continuously—no manual uploads needed.

  • Bank transactions
  • Invoice data from accounting software
  • Project/job cost information
  • Tenant accounting (if applicable)
2

AI Categorization

Machine learning handles 80% of data entry automatically.

Our proprietary AI has learned from thousands of construction and property management transactions. It instantly categorizes expenses, matches deposits to ledgers, and codes transactions to the right jobs or units.

  • Automatic expense categorization
  • Job/project coding
  • Rent posting to tenant accounts
  • Vendor payment matching
3

Expert Review

Real accountants verify every transaction for 100% accuracy.

Every categorization flagged by our AI review team is reviewed by a human accountant. We catch edge cases, verify compliance, and ensure your books are audit-ready. Your data is never trusted to AI alone.

  • Manual review of all transactions
  • Compliance verification
  • Audit preparation
  • Tax optimization recommendations
4

Monthly Strategy Call

We don't just do accounting—we help you grow.

Every month, your dedicated accountant reviews your financial data and shares insights: which projects are most profitable, where you're spending too much, tax optimization strategies, and opportunities to improve margins.

  • Profitability analysis
  • Tax planning
  • Cash flow projections
  • Growth recommendations
