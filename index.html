Welcome to DARES

We Offer a Full Range of Automated Bookkeeping Services

DARES combines cutting-edge automation technology with expert human accountants to deliver bookkeeping solutions you can trust. We serve construction companies and property management firms across multiple states.

Our specialized approach handles job costing, WIP reporting, unit-level analysis, trust account reconciliation, and complete tax preparation. With over two decades of combined experience, we understand the unique financial challenges your business faces.

Known for our hands-on approach, we build long-term client relationships rooted in trust, responsiveness, and expertise. Whether you're streamlining operations or addressing complex tax matters, DARES delivers smart, reliable solutions tailored to your needs.
